Our Company


La Candaya stands as a beacon of culinary artistry in Geniry, an ode to the city's rich gastronomic tapestry. Our chefs, dedicated to their craft, curate a menu that pays homage to tradition while embracing innovation. Nestled in the heart of the city, we source locally, ensuring each dish is a testament to the region's bountiful produce. From the sizzle of a perfectly seared steak to the delicate dance of flavors in a handcrafted dessert, every plate is a symphony of taste. Join us in this journey, and let your palate discover the true essence of our vibrant city

History


La Candaya, a culinary cornerstone in Geniry, boasts a storied history dating back to 1945. Founded by the visionary chef, Maria Rodriguez, it began as a modest eatery, serving local specialties. Over the decades, La Candaya evolved, becoming a symbol of culinary excellence in the region. Maria's commitment to sourcing the finest ingredients and her innovative approach to traditional recipes garnered acclaim. In the 1970s, her son, Juan Rodriguez, took the reins, infusing modern techniques while preserving his mother's legacy. The restaurant's reputation continued to flourish, attracting discerning diners and earning accolades. Today, under the stewardship of the Rodriguez family's third generation, La Candaya remains a beacon of gastronomic artistry, offering a timeless fusion of heritage and innovation. Its enduring legacy is a testament to the passion and dedication that have defined this cherished Geniry institution for over seven decades
